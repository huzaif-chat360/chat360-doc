Welcome to Chat360 Documentation’s documentation!¶


  1. Login in Chat360 or create a account - https://app.chat360.io/login


  2. Click on ADD NEW CHATBOT > START FROM STRATCH > Whatsapp


  3. Create the bot as per your need using side component panel and Save


  1. Now from Home Page > Channel > Whatsapp Testing > Add new Channel


  1. Add Details and your number and select your bot > create new channel


  1. After Channel created, you will get message on whatsapp, send msg to that number and your bot will start
