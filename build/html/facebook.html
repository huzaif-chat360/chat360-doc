How to configure your facebook page on chat360Â¶

  1. Go to https://app.chat360.io/login and Login or Register

  2. Click on ADD NEW CHATBOT > START FROM SCRATCH > Messenger

  1. This will open new tab with canvas editor

  2. Create a Simple Bot and Save it

  1. Now click on channel from side panel on main page - https://app.chat360.io/channel > Facebook > Add New Facebook

  1. Enter the details for Facebook channel and click on > Connect to Facebook

  1. Allow facebook App and Continue

  2. It will ask to select page on which you want to give permission. After select > Next > Done > OK

  1. Select the Fb page and click on Create Channel

  1. Now you will see your channel > click edit

  1. Now select the Facebook Bot and Change Unpublish to Publish and Save Changes

  1. Now your bot is ready, on your linked facebook page if anyone type message bot will respond
