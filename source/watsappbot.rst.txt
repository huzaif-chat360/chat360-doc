How to make a whatsapp bot on chatbot
==========================
1. Login in Chat360 or create a account - https://app.chat360.io/login

2. Click on ADD NEW CHATBOT > START FROM STRATCH > Whatsapp

3. Create the bot as per your need using side component panel and Save

.. figure:: /Picture1.jpg

4. Now from Home Page > Channel > Whatsapp Testing > Add new Channel

.. figure:: /Picture2.png

5. Add Details and your number and select your bot > create new channel

.. figure:: /Picture3.png

6. After Channel created, you will get message on whatsapp, send msg to that number and your bot will start
